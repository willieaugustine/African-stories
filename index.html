Students Riots

I’m deeply shocked at the behavior of some University of Nairobi students. Recently, they burnt down a kitchen and public address system facilities worth tens of millions of shillings, all because they were prohibited from cooking in their hostels. I smell a hidden agenda. The management should explore the possibility of external influence. Let’s not allow a few people destroy the global reputation that UoN has built over so many years.
